Welcome!

Greetings, football enthusiasts! Welcome to my project: Analysis and Visualization of International Football Results from 1872 to 2024. This platform serves as an insightful journey through the rich history of international football, combining historical data, statistical analysis, and interactive visualizations to unravel some fascinating patterns.

Here, you will explore a variety of analyses, such as:

  • The top countries hosting the highest number of football matches.
  • A deep dive into own goals and penalties that have shaped matches.
  • An analysis of the top 10 goal scorers of all time.
  • Insights into the countries scoring the most goals in international football.
  • Timing trends of goals across various matches.
  • A look at countries winning matches via penalty shootouts.

Whether you're a football fan, a data enthusiast, or someone curious about the sport's evolution, this project has something to captivate your interest. Dive into the data, enjoy the visualizations, and celebrate the beautiful game with me!
